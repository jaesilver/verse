Financial Year is the actual financial year for which you are filing your return and the Assessment Year will always be next year of financial year. For example, if you file return for F.Y. 2015-16, then the assessment year will be 2016-17.

E-Filing is mandatory in certain cases. For example, if your total income exceeds Rs. 5,00,000 p.a. or if you want to claim refund then you are compulsorily required to e-file your Income Tax Return.
